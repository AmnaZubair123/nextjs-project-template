Patient Information

Everything you need to know about your hospital stay

COVID-19 Guidelines

For the safety of all patients and staff:

  • Wear a mask at all times
  • Maintain social distancing
  • Use hand sanitizer stations
  • Follow screening procedures

Admission Process

  • Complete pre-admission forms
  • Provide insurance information
  • Present valid ID
  • Bring current medications
  • Arrive 30 minutes early

What to Bring

  • Insurance card
  • Personal care items
  • Comfortable clothing
  • List of medications
  • Medical history documents

Patient Rights

  • Privacy protection
  • Access to medical records
  • Informed consent
  • Quality care
  • Complaint resolution

Admission Steps

1

Pre-Registration

Complete all necessary forms and provide insurance information before your arrival.

2

Check-In

Visit our admission desk with your ID and required documents.

3

Medical Assessment

Meet with healthcare providers for initial evaluation.

4

Room Assignment

Get settled in your assigned room and meet your care team.
